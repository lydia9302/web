Goal

The CommSec Explore & Research project aims to identify best practices for redesigning the market research experience based on the existing design. The goal is to create a coherent, user-friendly, and responsive market research experience for CommSec retail investors, while developing flexible modules that can be integrated across various CommSec channels and platforms.
As part of this process, the existing designs will be analyzed and modified, using relevant data, to ensure they are ready for development.

Key Features

From global indices to Australia-specific insights, each feature was designed to visualize, compare, and connect market data, bridging global trends with local perspectives through a unified research experience.

WORLD INDICES
Track Global Markets at a Glance

Visualizing the performance of major global markets, enabling users to compare trends and assess economic conditions across regions, while serving as a gateway to deeper index insights.

SECTOR PERFORMANCE
Find the Leaders Behind Every Sector

A dynamic heatmap and top-movers view highlight sector performance, helping users identify emerging trends and investment opportunities without being overwhelmed by data.

MARKET EVENT CALENDAR
Stay Ahead of Every Market Moment

A unified calendar that surfaces key events — from earnings and IPOs to macro data — enabling users to scan, filter, and plan around what’s moving the market today and ahead.

CURRENCIES & COMMODITIES & BONDS
Read the Global Pulse in One Glance

Real-time currency, commodity, and bond insights compare Australian and global markets, helping users interpret economic signals and spot shifts across regions.

Designed to Adapt, Built to Perform

Optimized across devices, breakpoints, and color themes, the UI was designed in alignment with MUI’s component system, ensuring consistency and performance wherever users access financial data.

Design Process

A structured, iterative design process from research and definition to exploration and delivery, transforming insights into clear, actionable solutions.

01

Discover

Analyzed the existing platform and built proof-of-concept designs, while researching competitor experiences to uncover opportunities for improvement.

02

Define

Collaborated closely with the client through a creative brief exercise to define project goals, challenges, dependencies, and scope with clarity and alignment.

03

Explore

Developed wireframes and multiple UI directions aligned with defined goals, refining the concept through data-driven iterations and progressive detailing.

04

Deliver

Executed full design exploration and interactive prototypes, delivering final layouts, documentation, and implementation-ready assets for handoff.

Challenges

Every challenge was an opportunity to scale — here's how I approached it.

Unclear Scope & Platform Direction

The CommSec and CBA ecosystem included multiple websites and native apps, but the project scope was undefined. The goal was to design flexible modules that could live across all platforms seamlessly.

No Established UI System

CommSec’s new UI system was still under development, with no clear visual or structural standards to follow at the start of the project.

First Time Leading End-to-End

This was my first time leading a full project — managing structure, planning, and delivery independently from start to finish.
