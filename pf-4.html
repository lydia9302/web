Design Process

A structured and iterative approach that moved from clear requirement definition to refined visual exploration and production readiness, ensuring every design decision was grounded in real data, usability, and scalability for HKEX’s institutional audience.

01

Requirement Setup & Wireframing

Clarified data entitlements and user requirements from the start.
Early sketches were created for each user tier to align expectations and ensure a solid foundation before moving into design.

02

UI Exploration

Explored multiple design directions through iterative visual studies.
Since HKEX’s existing guides didn’t fully meet executive expectations, we established a new UI concept tailored to their needs and audience.

03

Production Ready

Defined complete UI specifications and data visualization standards.
As HKEX had limited experience with data-driven interfaces, we developed detailed specs and helped establish their design system foundation.

04

Final Design

Delivered high-fidelity designs ready for development, reflecting a clean and data-forward interface optimized for institutional users.
