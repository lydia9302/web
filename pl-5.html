Presto Runners!

Turning the everyday commute into a playful city run.

Year
2022
Idea Spark

How can we reduce the stress of public transportation?

Inspired by Toronto’s PRESTO system, this idea gamifies commuting through a connected runner experience.
By running, collecting, and competing with others in real time, players relieve stress, discover the city, and even help ease traffic congestion — one ride at a time.
